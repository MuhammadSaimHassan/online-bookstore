A Feast of Crows

A Feast for Crows, the fourth book in George R.R. Martin’s A Song of Ice and Fire series, explores the fragile state of Westeros following the devastating War of the Five Kings. The narrative shifts focus to political intrigue and rebuilding, with Cersei Lannister consolidating her power in King’s Landing, the Ironborn vying for dominance, and the Martells of Dorne pursuing secretive plans. Elsewhere, Brienne of Tarth searches for the missing Stark girls, and Samwell Tarly journeys to the Citadel to uncover knowledge that could change the realm's future. With its focus on the quieter aftermath of war, the novel paints a haunting picture of a fractured world struggling to find stability amid lingering chaos.
