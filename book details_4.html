Book details

Dune

Frank Herbert’s Dune series is a science fiction epic set in a distant future where powerful noble families vie for control of Arrakis, a desert planet and the only source of "melange," a substance granting extended life and prescience. The saga explores themes of politics, ecology, religion, and human evolution. Following the journey of Paul Atreides and his descendants, it delves into their struggles with power, destiny, and survival. Known for its complex world-building and philosophical depth, Dune remains a landmark in speculative fiction, blending adventure with profound social commentary.
